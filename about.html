About REVA Alumni Community

The REVA Alumni Community is the official community of the 35,000+ REVAites who have now made different parts of the world their home. We are continuously striving and aspiring to bring you all together, connect with your alma mater, friends and many friends like family.

The Alumni Community is the bridging gap between information providers and seekers. Whether you are looking to reconnect with your classmates, mentor students, need information on job opportunities, or simply want to stay updated with the developments at REVA University, this platform will be the communication channel.

We will connect you and your classmates, friends with a rich network of personal and professional development resources. You can also stay connected on latest developments at REVA like new programs, faculty-student achievements, volunteer opportunities, incubation programs, and several more. The Community belongs to each one of you. This is your stage.

From the Vice Chancellor's Desk
Dr. M. Dhanamjaya

Dear Alumni/Alumna, Welcome to the official alumni website of REVA University. In our concentrated efforts to reconnect, rebuild and renew our ties with our students’ community, the REVA Alumni website has been launched. As a reflection of REVA’s founding philosophy ‘Knowledge is Power’, through this community, we aim to empower each and every one who is a part of this network. Any individual looking for job opportunities, guidance on vocational skills and training, mentorship and voluntary works can post queries in this website. If any individual is interested to visit us as guest faculty, mentor to students, or any other collaboration, REVA will provide a platform. We are proud to inform that REVA has launched ‘Abhivriddhi’, a unique initiative to educate government school students and I welcome you all to be a part of this novel initiative. You can visit the website to receive all the latest updates about REVA University. Let us all take this opportunity to uplift the alumni community and stay connected with our friends, faculty and former batch mates.

From the Chancellors desk
Dr P Shyama Raju

Dear Alumni, Welcome to your Alma Mater. REVA University was established with a core vision of nurturing and mentoring students who will work towards the holistic development of a society. In this mission, imparting valuable ethical, moral and crucial life lessons was our driving force, apart from delivering quality education. Every time I hear a success story of any REVA student, I am reminded that we are in the right direction of achieving our goal and we have remained on our path that nurtures not just leaders, but complete individuals who uphold valuable life lessons. Our responsibility of building people of tomorrow continues and I urge you all to join hands with us to carry our mission forward. You can mentor students, post job opportunities (both seeking and offering), connect for academic queries, and touch base with all your former classmates, batch mates and friends. This website is envisages to bring back and celebrate all the old memories, while we all look for windows of opportunities for tomorrow. This stage is the perfect amalgamation of the past and the future. I wish to inform you all that your Alma Mater is proud of you. We celebrate your every small and big success!
